Mohammad Moltafet - About me

I received the M.Sc. degree (Ranked 1st) in communications engineering from Tarbiat Modares University, Tehran, Iran, in 2016, and the Ph.D. degree in communications engineering from the University of Oulu, Finland, in 2021. I was a Visiting Ph.D. Researcher at Linköping University, Sweden, in 2019, and a Postdoctoral Researcher at the University of Oulu, and the Technical University of Munich, Germany, in 2021 and 2022, respectively. Currently, I am a Postdoctoral Researcher at the University of California at Santa Cruz, USA.

Student Advising
  • December 2021–Present: Saeid Sadeghi-Vilni, Ph.D. Candidate, University of Oulu; research area: Information freshness for Ultra-reliable low-latency applications; jointly with Prof. Marian Codreanu; expected graduation: 2025.
  • October 2021–Present: Abolfazl Zakeri, Ph.D. Candidate, University of Oulu; research area: Reinforcement learning for semantic-aware communications; jointly with Prof. Marian Codreanu; expected graduation: 2024.
